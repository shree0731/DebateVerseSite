Module 4: Rebuttals and Counterarguments

In debating, rebuttals are your chance to respond directly to your opponent’s arguments. Effective rebuttals are logical, concise, and targeted. Counterarguments present a different viewpoint with supporting evidence to weaken the opponent’s position.
